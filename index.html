About Me

Hi there! I'm Ana Rivas, a passionate Graphic and Web Designer with a keen eye for creativity and a love for innovative web development. I believe in the power of design to convey emotions, tell stories and create meaningful experiences for users. With a solid foundation in HTML, CSS and Javascript, I strive to bring beautiful designs to life on the digital canvas.

I have over 15 years of experience in the design industry, during which I've had the privilege to work with a diverse range of clients, from small startups to established brands. My journey started with traditional graphic design, but as technology evolved, I immersed myself in the world of web development to combine my passion for aesthetics and functionality.

I thrive on challenges and enjoy finding innovative solutions to design and development hurdles. Throughout my career, I have fostered a collaborative approach, working closely with clients, developers, and cross-functional teams to bring ideas to fruition.
